Concept 1 — Clean

Design notes

Feel

Familiar and frictionless. Looks like a polished Apple app. Anyone who uses an iPhone will feel immediately comfortable. No learning curve.

Typography

System font (-apple-system / San Francisco). Matches iOS exactly. Clean hierarchy using weight and size, not colour.

Colour

White base with iOS blue as the only accent. Coloured dots for calendar events. Sparse use of colour means it doesn't compete with content.

For Imogen

Highest familiarity. Looks like Apple Notes and Reminders, which she already uses. Lowest risk of "this feels like a work app".

Trade-offs

Can feel cold or corporate if not carefully warmed up with content and copy. The fun layer (films, ideas) needs more visual weight to balance the utility sections.

Works best for

Task-heavy usage, shopping lists, quick scanning. Excellent readability in daylight. The "safe" choice.
