Font choice
Plus Jakarta Sans is clean, modern, and slightly warm — not corporate like Inter. The 800 weight display text has genuine character at heading sizes. Available on Google Fonts, loads in one request.
Warm neutrals
Both backgrounds use brown-tinted neutrals (#F6F2EE light, #121009 dark) rather than grey or blue. This keeps the palette consistent and means the terracotta accent never looks out of place.
System integration
Both modes use identical CSS custom properties. In Next.js, a single class on <html> switches the whole app. Follows the system setting automatically with a manual override. Zero extra component work.
