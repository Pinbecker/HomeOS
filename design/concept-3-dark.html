Concept 3 — Dark

Design notes

Feel

Sophisticated and calm. Great for evening use when you're picking a film or checking the week ahead. OLED-friendly (true black background saves battery). Feels premium.

Typography

DM Serif Display for headers (personality without being decorative), DM Sans for UI (clean, technical, highly readable at small sizes). Tight pairing — same type family.

Colour

Amber as primary accent (warm, not cold). Emerald for completion/positive. Indigo for events. Alert red stays familiar. Very controlled palette — each accent has one meaning.

The insurance screen

This is the killer feature demo: insurance policies, MOT dates, subscriptions — all in one place, with colour-coded urgency. This is what makes the app genuinely useful.

Trade-offs

Highest risk for Imogen if she doesn't like dark mode. Could add a light/dark toggle, but that doubles the design work. Consider offering this as the default with an optional light mode.

Best for

Evening film browsing, insurance/admin reference, the dashboard at night. If you add an auto light/dark mode that follows the system, you get both concepts 1 and 3 for free.
